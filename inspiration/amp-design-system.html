08

Design Principles

Core aesthetic principles that guide the Amp visual identity.

Editorial & Premium

The design draws from high-end editorial and magazine aesthetics. Generous whitespace, refined typography, and a sophisticated color palette create a premium feel befitting frontier technology.

Terminal Heritage

As a coding tool, Amp embraces its terminal roots through monospace typography, code-styled elements, and a color scheme reminiscent of classic terminal interfaces with a modern twist.

Warmth in Darkness

Rather than cold, stark dark mode, Amp uses warm teal backgrounds and cream/gold accents to create an inviting, sophisticated atmosphere that's easy on the eyes during long sessions.

Purposeful Animation

Motion is subtle and meaningfulâ€”enhancing understanding rather than decorating. Hover states provide clear feedback, transitions feel natural, and nothing moves without reason.
